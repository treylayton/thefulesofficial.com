How It Works

Built Like a Real Rules Official

The Rules of Golf are complex because they have to be. Designed for every possible scenario across every possible course, the rulebook reads like a legal document.

Real rules officials simplify that complexity by asking the right questions in the right order until the correct ruling emerges. The Rules Official works exactly the same way.
