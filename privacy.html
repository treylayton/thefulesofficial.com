Privacy Policy

Effective March 9, 2026

Overview

The Rules Official ("the App") is built with a simple privacy principle: we don't collect your data. The App runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no data transmission of any kind.

Information We Collect

None. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • No device identifiers or fingerprints
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or behavioral data
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent to us
  • No cookies, pixels, or tracking technologies

On-Device AI

On supported devices, the App offers AI-powered rulings using Apple's on-device Foundation Models. All AI processing occurs entirely on your device. No queries, prompts, or results are transmitted to any server -- ours or anyone else's.

Subscription and Payments

Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple and is never accessible to us. We do not receive or store your payment details, Apple ID, or any purchase-related personal information.

For information about how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or external services of any kind. The App makes no network requests.

Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no data from any user, it inherently complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. There is nothing to collect from users of any age.

Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to secure, breach, or compromise. All content generated by the App exists only on your device and is subject to your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Any material change to our data practices -- particularly any introduction of data collection -- would require a new version of the App with updated disclosures.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us at:

support@therulesofficial.com

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar privacy regulations, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or port. If you have concerns, contact us at the address above.
